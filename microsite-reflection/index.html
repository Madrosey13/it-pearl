The feed back that I recived when doing my project were the following:

  • Original header colors too contrasting
  • Fix typography errors
  • Add accent colors to the site
  • Add images to the site
  • Add spacing between images and image captions

Developing the site was a challenge in itself. There were times were I developed decision paralysis just trying to figure out what I wanted to add to the site. But in the end, I think I was able to put those changes into my site to make it look somewhat presentable.

Where I went well was the organiziation of information HTML wise and the design aspect of the site.
